Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 175Â°C (350Â°F). Grease a 9x13 inch pan or 9-inch round cake pan.

Step 2

In a small bowl, mix together granulated sugar, cinnamon, and 2 tablespoons flour. Set aside.

Step 3

In a large bowl, mix together all cake ingredients until just combined and smooth. Don't overmix.

Step 4

Pour half the batter into prepared pan. Sprinkle half the cinnamon mixture over the batter. Add remaining batter on top, then sprinkle with remaining cinnamon mixture.

Step 5

Use a knife to gently swirl the cinnamon mixture through the batter in a figure-8 or zigzag pattern. Don't overmix.

Step 6

Bake for 30-35 minutes for rectangular pan, or 35-40 minutes for round pan, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out with just a few moist crumbs.

Step 7

Cool in pan for 10 minutes before serving.
