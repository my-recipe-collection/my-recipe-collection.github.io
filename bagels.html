Instructions

Step 1

Mix warm water, yeast, and 1 teaspoon sugar. Let rest for 5 minutes until foamy.

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine flour, remaining sugar, and salt. Make a well in the center.

Step 3

Add yeast mixture and oil. Knead until smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.

Step 4

Cover and let rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.

Step 5

Shape into bagels.

Step 6

Let rise for another 30 minutes.

Step 7

Boil water with 1 tablespoon sugar. Add bagels for 30 seconds per side. Add toppings.

Step 8

Bake at 250Â°C (480Â°F) for 15-20 minutes.
