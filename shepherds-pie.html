Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 200g mushrooms, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 8 potatoes (can add a few sweet potatoes)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a large pan. Fry the diced onion, celery, mushrooms, carrot and garlic until soft.

Step 2

Add ground beef and brown it well.

Step 3

Mix everything together, then add tomato puree and water.

Step 4

Add thyme, parsley, salt, pepper and soy sauce.

Step 5

Cook until everything is soft and dark in color.

Step 6

Meanwhile, boil potatoes (and sweet potatoes if using) in salted water until tender.

Step 7

Mash the potatoes with vegetable oil, salt and pepper.

Step 8

Place meat mixture in bottom of a 9x13 inch foil dish.

Step 9

Cover with mashed potatoes, use a fork to make lines on top, and spray or brush with vegetable oil.

Step 10

Bake at 200Â°C (400Â°F) until golden brown on top.
