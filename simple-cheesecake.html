Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.

Step 2

Crush biscuits finely and mix with melted butter. Press firmly into the base of the pan.

Step 3

Mix cream cheese and sugar until just smooth. Don't overmix.

Step 4

Gradually stir in milk, eggs, sour cream, and vanilla until combined.

Step 5

Add flour and mix until just smooth. Pour over the crust.

Step 6

Optional: Wrap pan in foil and place in a water bath to prevent cracking.

Step 7

Bake for 1 hour. Center should still be slightly wobbly.

Step 8

Turn off oven and leave cheesecake inside for 2 hours.

Step 9

For topping: Heat berries with sugar and water. When bubbling, add cornstarch solution and cook until thickened.

Step 10

Cool topping slightly before spreading over cheesecake. Chill until serving.
