Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 1 package (250g) Petit Beurre biscuits
  • 100g melted butter

For the Filling:

  • 3 packages (750g) white cheese 9%
  • 1 container (200ml) sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 170Â°C (340Â°F).

Step 2

Crush the biscuits in a food processor and mix with melted butter.

Step 3

Press the mixture into the bottom of a 24cm (9.5 inch) springform pan.

Step 4

Mix all filling ingredients in a mixer until smooth.

Step 5

Pour over the base.

Step 6

Bake for 45 minutes or until set but still slightly wobbly in the center.

Step 7

Cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
