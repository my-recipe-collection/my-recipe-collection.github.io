Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F).

Step 2

Beat oil, brown sugar, and white sugar for about 3 minutes. Add vanilla extract, coffee, and baking soda. Mix well.

Step 3

Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition until smooth and creamy.

Step 4

Add flour and mix until just combined.

Step 5

Scoop dough using a medium cookie scoop. First roll in granulated sugar (optional, but helps the powdered sugar show better). Then roll in powdered sugar until fully coated. Place on baking sheet with space between cookies.

Step 6

Bake for 9-10 minutes until edges are set.
