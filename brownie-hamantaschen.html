Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups 80% spelt flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Brownie Filling:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup 80% spelt flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

For the Chocolate Drizzle:

  • 1 bag chocolate chips

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step 2

For the dough: Mix oil, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Add flour, baking powder, and salt and mix to form a smooth dough. Refrigerate.

Step 3

For the filling: Mix sugar, oil, egg, and vanilla. Add flour, cocoa powder, instant coffee, and salt and mix until smooth.

Step 4

Roll out dough to 0.5 cm thickness and cut 6 cm circles.

Step 5

Place 1 teaspoon of filling in the center of each circle. Fold into triangles and pinch corners.

Step 6

Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly golden on the bottom.

Step 7

Melt chocolate chips and drizzle over cooled cookies.
