Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups 80% spelt flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Brownie Filling:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup 80% spelt flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

For the Chocolate Drizzle:

  • 1 bag chocolate chips, melted

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F) and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step 2

To make the dough: In a large bowl, mix oil, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir until a smooth dough forms. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate while preparing the filling.

Step 3

To make the brownie filling: Using the same bowls, combine sugar, oil, egg, and vanilla in the wet bowl. In the dry bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, instant coffee, and salt. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir until smooth.

Step 4

Remove dough from the fridge. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to about 0.5 cm thickness. Cut circles using a 6 cm cookie cutter. Re-roll scraps to use all dough.

Step 5

Place 1 teaspoon of brownie filling in the center of each circle. Fold the dough from three sides, pinching the corners to seal, leaving a small opening in the middle. Transfer to baking sheets and bake for 10-12 minutes, until bottoms are lightly golden. Cool on a wire rack.

Step 6

For the chocolate drizzle: Melt the bag of chocolate chips in the microwave or using a double boiler. Drizzle over cooled hamantaschen and let set for 20 minutes.
