Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • Sprinkles for decorating

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F (180Â°C). Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step 2

Mix together the oil and sugar in a medium bowl. Add the vanilla and eggs. Mix well.

Step 3

Add the baking soda, salt, flour, and corn starch. Mix until a dough forms. It may seem floury at first, but keep mixing and it will all combine into a cookie dough.

Step 4

Use a tablespoon or small cookie scoop to spoon the cookies onto the baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches between each cookie. Top with sprinkles.

Step 5

Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are lightly golden.
