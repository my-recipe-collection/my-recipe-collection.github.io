Instructions

Step 1

Grate the potatoes until they are almost pulp-like in consistency.

Step 2

Place grated potatoes in a sieve and let drain for 10 minutes.

Step 3

Transfer to a bowl and add all remaining ingredients.

Step 4

Heat oil in a heavy frying pan to a depth of Â½ inch.

Step 5

When oil is hot, drop tablespoons of the mixture into the pan, flattening each latke with the back of the spoon.

Step 6

Cook over moderate heat for 5 minutes on each side, or until brown.

Step 7

Drain on kitchen paper before serving.
