Ingredients

  • 5-6 courgettes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil or 1 tablespoon fresh basil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • Optional: black olives, cut up
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

Instructions

Step 1

Cut courgettes into 1 inch (2.5cm) slices.

Step 2

Heat oil in a large pan and cook onions until golden and soft.

Step 3

Add all ingredients except courgettes and cook gently for 5 minutes.

Step 4

Add courgettes, cover and simmer for 10 minutes until courgettes are tender.

Step 5

If sauce is too thin, continue cooking uncovered until it thickens.

Step 6

Optional: Add cut up black olives and chopped parsley before serving.
