Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest (from 2 lemons)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice (about 1/2 lemon)
  • 1 cup water

For the Lemon Syrup:

  • 1/2 cup lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
  • 6 tablespoons powdered sugar

For the Lemon Glaze:

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons water

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F (180Â°C). Grease two loaf pans.

Step 2

In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, lemon zest, and salt.

Step 3

In a large bowl, mix oil and sugar well.

Step 4

Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

Step 5

Add vanilla and lemon juice.

Step 6

Gradually add the flour mixture alternating with water, beginning and ending with flour.

Step 7

Divide batter evenly between the two pans.

Step 8

Bake for 45-55 minutes until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.

Step 9

Cool for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, make the syrup by mixing lemon juice and powdered sugar.

Step 10

Remove from pans carefully and brush with syrup while still warm.

Step 11

Allow to cool completely.

Step 12

Mix glaze ingredients, starting with 1 tablespoon each of lemon juice and water, adding more gradually until desired consistency.

Step 13

Pour glaze over cooled cakes and let set before serving.
