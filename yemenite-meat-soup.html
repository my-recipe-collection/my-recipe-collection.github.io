Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons canola oil
  • Beef stew cubes
  • 1 onion, peeled and diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled and sliced thinly
  • 2 teaspoons hawaij spice mixture
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cut in rounds
  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2-3 russet potatoes, peeled and cut in large chunks

Instructions

Step 1

Heat the oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add the beef cubes, stir, cover and cook for about 10 minutes, until the meat and bones are somewhat browned.

Step 2

Uncover the pot and add the diced onions, garlic, hawaij and tomato paste. Stir to distribute evenly and cook for about 5 minutes to deepen the tomato flavor.

Step 3

Add the carrots and stock. Cover the pot and simmer on a low flame for one hour.

Step 4

Add the potatoes, cover the soup again and cook for an additional hour.

Step 5

Discard the bones and serve hot.
