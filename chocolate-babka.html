Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 7 cups (1 kg) all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp dry yeast
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cup water

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 2 cups Dutch cocoa
  • 4 cups sugar
  • 2 cups confectioners sugar
  • 4 Tbsp vanilla sugar
  • 1 pkg instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 6 Tbsp hot water

For the Crumble:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla sugar
  • 1/3 cup oil

Instructions

Step 1

Mix all dough ingredients in a mixer with dough hook for 10 minutes.

Step 2

Let rest for 5 minutes, then knead again for 5 minutes.

Step 3

Let dough rise for about 10 minutes.

Step 4

Divide dough into 3 parts.

Step 5

For the chocolate filling, mix all dry ingredients together. Remove 2 cups and leave aside for later. Mix with additional oil and hot water until spreadable.

Step 6

For the crumble, mix all ingredients until crumbly. Add more oil if needed.

Step 7

Roll each piece very thin until you can see through it.

Step 8

Spread with wet then dry chocolate filling and roll up tightly.

Step 9

Twist into babka shape and place in greased pan.

Step 10

Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle crumble on top.

Step 11

Bake at 350Â°F (180Â°C) for 45 minutes.
