Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 large carrots, shredded
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baharat spice blend
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup toasted sliced almonds

Instructions

Step 1

Rinse the basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a heavy-bottomed large pan over medium heat.

Step 3

SautÃ© the onion until golden brown, about 5 minutes.

Step 4

Add garlic and shredded carrots, cook for 3 more minutes until softened.

Step 5

Add cumin, cinnamon, and baharat. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.

Step 6

Add the rinsed rice and stir for 3 minutes to lightly toast the grains.

Step 7

If using raisins, add them now and stir briefly.

Step 8

Pour in hot water, add honey, salt, and pepper. Stir once gently.

Step 9

Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover tightly.

Step 10

Cook for 18 minutes on low heat without lifting the lid.

Step 11

Remove from heat and let rest covered for 10 minutes.

Step 12

Fluff with a fork, sprinkle with toasted almonds, and serve.
