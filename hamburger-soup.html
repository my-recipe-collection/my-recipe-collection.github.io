Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup finely diced onion
  • 2 carrots, cut into cubes
  • 2 celery stalks, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 kg lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (225g) tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning (or 1/3 tsp each: dried garlic powder, dried oregano, dried basil)
  • 1.5 liters water
  • 1 large potato, cubed (or 1 cup small pasta shapes like orzo or small shells)
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley

Instructions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery.

Step 2

Cook for 5-6 minutes until vegetables are softened.

Step 3

Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper. Cook while breaking up the meat until browned.

Step 4

Add garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.

Step 5

Add tomatoes, sauce, seasoning, and water. If using potatoes, add them now. If using pasta, wait until step 6.

Step 6

If using potatoes, cook for 25-30 minutes until tender. If using pasta, add it now and cook according to package instructions (usually 8-10 minutes). Adjust seasoning.

Step 7

Add corn and green beans, cook for 5 more minutes. Sprinkle with dried parsley and serve.
