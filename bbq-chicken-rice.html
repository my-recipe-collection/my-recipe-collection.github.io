Ingredients

For the Rice Base:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 14 chicken pieces

For the BBQ Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp silan (date syrup)
  • 1/2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp chipotle powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 220Â°C (425Â°F).

Step 2

Spread basmati rice evenly in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour water over rice and give it a gentle stir.

Step 3

In a bowl, whisk together all BBQ sauce ingredients until well combined.

Step 4

Arrange chicken pieces on top of the rice, distributing evenly. Pour the BBQ sauce over the chicken and rice.

Step 5

Cover tightly with foil and bake for 45 minutes.

Step 6

Remove foil and bake another 15-20 minutes until chicken is golden and crispy, and rice has absorbed most of the liquid.
