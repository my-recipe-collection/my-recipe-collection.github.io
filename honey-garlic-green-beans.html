Ingredients

  • Olive oil for cooking
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 package frozen green beans

For the Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
