Ingredients

For the Apple Filling:

  • 5 apples, peeled and diced small
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 packet (10g) vanilla sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups (420g) 80% spelt flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 packet (10g) vanilla sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup (240ml) vegetable oil
  • 1-3 tablespoons water as needed

Instructions

Step 1

Heat a large pan over high heat. Add diced apples, lemon juice, vanilla sugar, regular sugar, and honey. Mix and cook covered for 8 minutes, then uncovered for 7 more minutes until apples soften and liquid evaporates. Remove from heat and let cool.

Step 2

Mix all dough ingredients in a bowl: flour, sugar, vanilla sugar, egg, and oil. Add water gradually as needed to form a smooth dough. Reserve half the dough in the freezer.

Step 3

Preheat oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F). Grease a 24cm round pan.

Step 4

Press half the dough into the bottom and sides of the pan (about 1cm up the sides). Poke holes with a toothpick and bake for 12 minutes.

Step 5

Spread the cooled apple mixture evenly over the pre-baked crust.

Step 6

Remove reserved dough from freezer and crumble evenly over the apples.

Step 7

Return to oven and bake for 35 minutes until golden brown. Cool completely before cutting.
