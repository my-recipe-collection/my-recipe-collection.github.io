Instructions

Step 1

Mix yeast with warm water and sugar. Let stand for 5 minutes until foamy.

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.

Step 3

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Mix until a dough forms.

Step 4

Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Step 5

Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.

Step 6

Divide into 10-12 equal portions and shape into balls.

Step 7

Roll each ball into a 8-inch circle for personal pizzas.

Step 8

Add tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings of your choice.

Step 9

Bake at 425Â°F (220Â°C) for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
