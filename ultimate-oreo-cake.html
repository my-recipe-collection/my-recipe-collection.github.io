Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 28 Oreo cookies - roughly chopped
  • 4.67 cups (600g) 80% spelt flour
  • 1/2 cup (50g) cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter - room temperature
  • 1 cup (210g) vegetable oil
  • 3.33 cups (664g) white sugar
  • 6 large eggs - room temperature
  • 2.5 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp white vinegar
  • 3 cups (674g) buttermilk - room temperature

For the Oreo Buttercream:

  • 28 Oreo cookies - blended to fine crumbs
  • 3 cups (680g) unsalted butter - room temperature
  • 5.5 cups (700g) powdered sugar
  • 3 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp milk

Instructions

For the Cake:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 160Â°C (320Â°F) with fan. Grease two 8-inch cake pans.

Step 2

Roughly chop 14 Oreos into small chunks. Set aside.

Step 3

In a bowl, sift together flour, cornstarch, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.

Step 4

In another bowl, cream butter, oil, and sugar with an electric mixer for 2 minutes until light and fluffy.

Step 5

Add eggs one by one, mixing well between each addition.

Step 6

Add vanilla, vinegar, and 1/2 cup buttermilk. Mix until combined.

Step 7

Gently fold in half the dry ingredients by hand, then remaining buttermilk, then remaining dry ingredients. Don't overmix.

Step 8

Fold in chopped Oreos. Divide batter between pans and bake 33 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.

For the Buttercream:

Step 9

Blend 14 Oreos in food processor until fine crumbs.

Step 10

Beat butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk for 10 minutes until fluffy. Fold in Oreo crumbs.

Assembly:

Step 11

Place first layer on serving plate. Spread buttercream on top and sprinkle with 3 crushed Oreos.

Step 12

Add second layer and frost entire cake. Pipe swirls on top and press Oreo crumbs around sides. Top with whole Oreos.
