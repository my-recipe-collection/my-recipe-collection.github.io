Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons yeast
  • 5 cups warm water
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2kg "80% spelt" flour plus about 4 cups more as needed
  • 2 tablespoons salt

Instructions

Step 1

In a large bowl, combine yeast, warm water and all the sugar. Let proof 5 minutes.

Step 2

Add eggs, honey and oil. Mix well.

Step 3

Add flour and salt. Start with 2kg flour and knead in the bowl, gradually adding more flour (usually about 4 cups more) until the dough is soft and just stops sticking to your hands. Continue kneading for 10 minutes.

Step 4

Cover well. Let rise in a warm, draft-free spot until doubled in size, about 1 hour.

Step 5

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180Â°C).

Step 6

"Take challah", then portion and braid your challahs. Allow to rise at least 10 minutes or until visibly puffed. Brush with egg wash, sprinkle any toppings and bake until golden brown.
